Alexandria Dockless Mobility Pilot Evaluation

Key Project Details

  • Client: City of Alexandria, VA
  • Role: Project Manager
  • Date: November, 2019
  • Link: Full Report

Description

Managed this comprehensive evaluation of the City of Alexandria's shared e-scooter pilot program. I proposed the evaluation approach and coordinated the collection and analysis of ridership, demographic, cost, revenue, and safety data. After assessing the the pilot's impacts across the community, I proposed policy changes for the continuation of the program, including new geographic distribution requirements for scooter deployment based on the results of our equity analyis. I coordinated an independent injury report from the health department to clearly understand the program's safety impacts. Managed project timeline, budget, and deliverable.

Credit

This project represents a collective effort by many people at Toole Design Group and partners at the Alexandria Department of Transportation & Environmental Services. I did not create the report layout and graphics.
