Project Details

Winning entry to the 2020 ITE Micromobility Design Competition. Included demographic, economic, and transportation research on the study corridor, neighborhood, and city; multimodal roadway reallocation; safety treatment selection; and revitalization planning.

Project Role

Multimodal Transportation Planner

Work Performed

  • Led all planning aspects of winning design.
  • Conducted economic, demographic, and safety analysis.
  • Graphically communicated engineering design using Adobe Illustrator.
  • Designed and implemented report layout in Adobe InDesign.
  • Wrote report sections and served as an editor responsible for final competition entry submission.
