Project Information

  • Category: Design Guidelines
  • Client: City of Virginia Beach
  • Project date: February, 2021
  • Project URL: www.vbgov.com

Project Details

A singificant update from the City's previous design standards, these active transportation design guidelines encourage consistent implementation of safer and more comfortable bicycle and pedestrian facilities on City streets.

Project Role

Multimodal transportation planner

Work Performed

  • Crafted state-of-practice guidelines for use in Virginia Beach.
  • Curated and adapted context-sensitive content from AASHTO, NACTO, FHWA, and other leading national design guidance resources.
  • Coordinated guideline production with client.
  • Edited final guidelines based on client feedback.
